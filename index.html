Boost Your Writing Career

1. Own Your Space

  • Secure a real domain name (for example, ampboxingclub.com or yournamewrites.com).
  • Host your own content independently to signal professionalism and maintain full control over your work.

2. Design with Purpose

A simple late-90s retro style can work—if it’s deliberate and crafted well. Otherwise, it risks looking unpolished.

Key elements to include:

  • A concise, compelling bio that tells your story.
  • A clean headshot or a distinctive graphic logo.
  • Clear mention of your publishing house along with your Library and Archives Canada registration.
  • A direct link to your book on Amazon, ideally complemented with a quote or blurb from a reviewer.
  • A complete archive of your writing portfolio, not just a limited sample.

3. Make It Easy to Connect

  • Provide a straightforward contact form or an email address (protected against spam bots).
  • Indicate your availability for freelance projects, collaborations, or commissions if that’s part of your goals.
