About

Anthony Michael Perri is a Canadian writer and independent publisher. His work spans fiction, poetry, and essay, with a focus on the inner world of outsiders, forgotten cities, and failed idealisms. He is the founder of AMP Publishing House, officially registered with Library and Archives Canada.
